PennApps XIII

Started in the spring of 2009, PennApps is the nations's premier semiannual college hackathon run by students at University of Pennsylvania.

Built upon the idea that everyone should be able to participate hackathon, PennApps introduced applications for "Hacker Gurus" to pair with teams of new hackers spending the weekend together so every attendee can learn something new, build something novel and start something from scratch.
